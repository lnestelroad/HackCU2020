food waste is a *problem*,

  • Between 30% - 40% of the United States' food supply is wasted
  • Of the estimated 125 - 160 billion pounds of food that goes to waste every year, much of it is perfectly edible and nutritous
  • Food waste costs the United States $218 billion every year
  • An estimated 43 billion pounds of food were wasted in the US retail stores in 2010. Most of the loss in retail operations is in perishables, including baked goods, produce, meat, seafood and prepared meals. The USDA estimates that supermarkets lose $15 billion annually in unsold fruit and vegetables alone
  • The main drivers of food loss at retail stores include overstocked product displays, expectation of cosmetic perfection of fruits, oversized packaging, the availability of prepared food until closing, expired “sell by” dates, and over purchasing of unpopular foods
  • US restauarants generate an estimated 22 - 33 billion pounds of food waste each year
  • Schools, hotels, and hospitals generate an additional 7 - 11 billion pounds of wasted food every year

and there are people in *need*,

  • 1 in 6 American children live in a state of food insecurity, not knowing where their next meal will come from
  • 12% of American households are food insecure
  • There are approximately 50,000,000 food insecure people in the United States, and 800,000,000 food insecure people globally

    • What does food insecure mean?

    "A economic and social condition of limited or uncertian access to adequate food." -USDA

but we have a *solution*.

  • Our app provides an easy way for restauarants, schools, hotels, hospitals, and grocery stores to deliver their extra food to local food banks
  • If you are a restauarant, school, hotel, hospital, or grocery store, all you have to do is say what extra food you are willing to donate
  • If you are a food bank, all you have to do is say what food you need
  • Crowdsourced drivers volunteer to pickup and deliver extra food
  • We take care of all of the logistics, and prepare the most efficient driving route to pickup extra food and deliver it to food banks in need

People and Earth will love it too :)

  • 72% of US diners care about how restauarants handle food waste
  • 47% of US diners would be willing to spend more to eat at a restaurant with an active food recovery program
  • Reducing food waste by just 15% could provide enough sustenance to feed more than 25 million people annually
  • Only five percent of food is composted in the US and as a result, uneaten food is the single largest component of municipal solid waste
  • In landfills, food gradually breaks down to form methane, a greenhouse gas that’s up to 86 times more powerful than carbon dioxide. According to a report from the UK based organization WRAP, if food were removed from UK landfills, the greenhouse gas abatement would be equivalent to removing one-fifth of all the cars in the UK from the road
